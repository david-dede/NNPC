MARCH 27, 2024
CHIEF CORPORATE COMMUNICATIONS OFFICER, NNPC LTD, OLUFEMI SONEYE BAGS NIPR'S DISTINGUISHED SPOKESPERSON IN OIL & GAS AWARD

President,  Africa Public Relations Association, Mr. Yomi Badejo-Okusanya (left) presents the Distinguished Spokesperson in Oil and Gas Award to the Chief Corporate Communications Officer, Mr. Olufemi Soneye, represented by the Head, Relationship & Stakeholder Management,  Mrs. Oluwakemi Olumuyiwa, during the National Spokespersons Awards, in Abuja, on Wednesday. Standing from the right are the Manager, Media Relations, Mr. Aliyu Abubakar and Mrs. Olumuyiwa's daughter.

President,  Africa Public Relations Association, Mr. Yomi Badejo-Okusanya (4th from left) presents the Distinguished Spokesperson in Oil and Gas Award to the Chief Corporate Communications Officer, Mr. Olufemi Soneye, represented by the Head, Relationship & Stakeholder Management,  Mrs. Oluwakemi Olumuyiwa (5th from right), during the National Spokespersons Awards, in Abuja, on Wednesday.

President,  Africa Public Relations Association, Mr. Yomi Badejo-Okusanya (right) applauds as the the Chief Corporate Communications Officer, Mr. Olufemi Soneye was announced winner of the Distinguished Spokesperson in Oil and Gas Award in Abuja, on Wednesday. Standing next to him is the Chairman/CEO of the Award Co-organisers, Image Merchants Ltd, Mr. Yushau Shuaib.

President, Nigerian Institute of Public Relations (NIPR), Dr. Ike Neliaku congratulates the Chief Corporate Communications Officer, Mr. Olufemi Soneye, represented by the Head, Relationship & Stakeholder Management, Mrs. Oluwakemi Olumuyiwa (5th from right), shortly after conferment of the Distinguished Spokesperson in Oil & Gas Award on Mr. Soneye, during the National Spokespersons Awards, in Abuja, on Wednesday. Standing on Mr. Badejo-Okusanya's right and Mrs. Olumuyiwa's left are staff of the Corporate Communications Division.

L-R, Mrs. Oluwakemi Olumuyiwa, (Head, Relationship & Stakeholder Management), with Bola Olukoju (Commissioner for Communications) representative of Kwara State Governor.

A Group photograph of the awardees after the National Awards ceremony, in Abuja, on Wednesday.
